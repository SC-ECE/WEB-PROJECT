Welcome to Joy Makers

Joy Makers exists to alleviate poverty by enabling the world to give. Established in 2000, Joy Makers, together with its partners, is the largest and most trusted giving platform in India. Joy Makers enables individuals and organizations to raise and donate funds conveniently to any cause they care about, with offerings including crowdfunding, corporate giving, cause marketing, and philanthropy consulting. Joy Makers community of 2.6M+ donors supports 2,800+ verified nonprofits, serving 15M+ people across the country.
